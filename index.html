Frequently Asked Questions

Venue 1420 is the ultimate flexible, open floorplan space for your creative events.

Q.

How big is Venue 1420?

Venue 1420 is an open, flexible space with over 2,200 square feet for your special events. Venue 1420 can hold up to 200 people.

Q.

Where is Venue 1420 located?

Venue 1420 is located in the heart of the south loop in Chicago on world-renowned Michigan Ave. Our address is 1420 South Michigan Ave.

Q.

How personalized can i make the space for my event?

Within the WorkVault family, we champion creativity, love and innovation. We work with you to create and execute your very own inspired event masterpiece.

Q.

Is there parking available?

Street parking is available and valet parking can be added for an additional fee.
