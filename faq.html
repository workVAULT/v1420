FAQ

Q.
Catering?

Please ask us for our preferred vendor listing. Outside catering for food service is allowed in our space.

Q.
Am I responsible for clean-up?

Venue1420 will professionally clean the space before and after your event: sweeping, mopping, detailing the bathroom, and taking care of any necessary upkeep. You (or your caterer) are responsible for: Breaking down all boxes, bagging all trash Breaking down all rentals and placing in designated location in the venue for pick-up Removing all outside/3rd party materials from the premises (gifts, guest book, signage, etc) Basically, leave our space free of debris & clutter.

Q.
Is there any outdoors or rooftop space available?

Currently, we do not have a roof or garden area

Q.
Does the building have heat and A/C?

Yes.

Q.
How do I book a date?

Through Venue1420 website. Please see the Book it tab

Q.
How many restrooms do you have?

Venue1420 has two unisex restrooms in the event space

Q.
What is the cancellation policy?

If you cancel within 30 calendar days of the event, you will be charged 50% of Rental Fee as a cancellation fee.

Q.
Is smoking allowed?

No.

Q.
Do I need to provide my own insurance?

We require all outside vendors coming into the space to provide a certificate of insurance.

Q.
Is the building wheelchair accessible?

Yes.

Q.
How late can we stay?

This depends on how long you rent the venue. The end time of your event will be indicated in your contract. 11pm is last call.

Q.
How much time should I allow for my vendors to set-up and tear-down?

Your caterer, vendor or planner will be able to advise you.

Q.
Can I decorate the space?

Yes! You are welcome to customize the venue to your needs. We only ask that you leave it as you found it. If you do decide to utilize the walls by hanging things or for decoration purposes, we require that you have approval from us about your plans beforehand the day of your event.

Q.
Where can I have my vendors drop of rentals etc.?

Your vendors can load in & out the same day of your event. We allow access to the back alley entrance to the venue prior to your rental time as long as you clear it with a Venue1420 representative and you have a person at 1420 to receive the delivery.

Q.
Do I have a required food and beverage minimum?

No. Venue1420 has a rental fee for the space. Food and beverage is worked out with your caterer.

Q.
Can we bring our own alcohol?

Yes. We require that you have a licensed individual serving. Venue1420 does not allow for a self-service bar. If you choose to go this route, you will need to obtain a banquet permit and provide insurance for your event.

Q.
What if I exceed the time booked?

You will know in advance if you have a event following yours. In that case, your event cannot run long. If you exceed you total hours booked, you will be charged for the additional hours, including tear-down time. $225.00 per hour. You need to be cleaned up & completely out of the space by 12 midnight.

Q.
What technology is available?

HD Projection & Screen 3 Speakers

Q.
Where do my guests park?

Street parking or local garage parking. You can hire the valet service

Q.
Is security personnel provided or required?

One (1) licensed contracted security person is required for over 150 guests, when alcohol is served. Security personnel are not provided by the venue.

Q.
Are candles allowed?

All candles must be contained in glass with bottoms. Open flames are not allowed anywhere in the venue. No candles with a flame are permitted on the floor of the venue. Battery operated candles are suggested.

Q.
Is there a dressing area/green room on-site?

No.

Q.
How many floors are available for my event?

Venue1420 is located in a unique facility. The building’s basement and 2nd floor houses private professional offices. To protect the office members’ space, we do not allow access to the basement or the second floor of the facility. Only the 1st/main floor is available for events.
